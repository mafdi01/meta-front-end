Austin Born & Bred

The Vision of Mariana Gallo

From a local design school graduate to a master diamond cutter, Mariana has spent her life perfecting the art of the "classic look."

The Atelier

Located in the heart of Austin, our storefront serves as a gateway to Mariana's attached atelier. While we primarily operate online, this physical space allows clients to witness the high-quality craftsmanship of our artisans firsthand.

Our Process & Team

What started as a one-woman operation has grown into a collaborative hub. Mariana now leads a team of specialized artisans who help bring her intricate metal-smithing designs to life, ensuring every anniversary and wedding piece meets our "classic gold" standard.

  • Design: Mariana's signature hand-drawn concepts.
  • Craft: Collaborative metal-smithing by our expert artisans.
  • Ethos: High-quality, conflict-free, and timeless.

Handcrafted elegance, born in Austin.
